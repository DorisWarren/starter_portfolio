Who am i?

Good question. I am on a crazy journey trying to figure that one out. It is a constant flux of change everyday. It has been a fun ride so far. I can tell you my name is Doris Warren. I am 25 years old. One of my favorite memories as a child was riding my bike around. Getting lost in the orange groves. Staring out at the mountains during golden hour. Soaking in peachy sunsets. The moments of peace between my mind and life. It taught me to become still, appreciate my surroundings. I grew up on a ranch in Palm Springs Ca. We grow dates, oranges, tangerines, figs, guavas and mangos. I was born and raised in this hot, sunny land of palm trees. I moved to portland about a year ago. Being here has made me realize i will always be a sun bathing lizzard! Lover of animal, plants,art, and music.
